MISSING COURSES IN SPeCTRUM??

MISSING COURSES IN SPeCTRUM / RECENTLY CHANGE OCCURRENCE/GROUP?

Dear students, please make sure you have completed the Confirmation of Registration in the MAYA portal to ensure that your courses remain in SPeCTRUM.


Missing courses will reappear on SPeCTRUM within 24 hours after the Confirmation of Registration.


Alternatively, you can click this button Get Course(s) from MAYA or on the right navigation bar.


If any of your courses are missing or wrong group, please follow these steps:


  1. Go to Home / Dashboard / My Courses
  2. Click the GET COURSE(S) FROM MAYA button in the side drawer.
  3. Select the appropriate Semester and Session.
  4. Click UPDATE INTO SPECTRUM — this step is mandatory.
  5. Go to the My Courses page to view your course list. Make sure the Course Overview filter is set to All.

* Note: this function is now also available for the lecturers.
** Not applicable for term based courses (Faculty of Medicine Undergraduate Program)

When? After completing Confirmation of Registration in MAYA portal, use this function if your courses do not appear within 24 hours.


How?
1. Go to Home / Dashboard / My Courses
